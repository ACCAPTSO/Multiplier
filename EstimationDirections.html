Does your calculator lie to you?
Perhaps you mistyped something.
Perhaps your batteries are going dead.
Perhaps it's just messing with you.
The important thing is, is it telling you the truth?

It may be easier to tell than you think!

In this exercise you will be given an arithmetic problem
and four statements about that problem.

One or two of the statements are true.

For each of the four statements,
select "True" or "Possible" or leave "False",
then click "Check".

Click "Next Problem" to continue
